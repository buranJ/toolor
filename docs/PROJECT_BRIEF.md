# Project brief

TOOLOR is a new e-commerce foundation for a contemporary clothing brand from Kyrgyzstan. The working positioning is “community of modern nomads”: mountains, nature, movement and a present-day nomadic lifestyle expressed through a restrained premium fashion interface.

Stage one delivers a production-shaped frontend without a real commerce backend, authentication, payment or cinematic motion runtime. Its purpose is to validate information architecture, visual direction, accessibility, performance boundaries and backend seams.

The homepage is static and fully usable today. Its independent semantic sections expose scroll anchors and a motion slot so a later frame-by-frame canvas experience can be attached without replacing navigation or content.

Product records and prices are generated from the supplied workbook. They feed a mock provider and are not a live offer; availability, delivery and checkout remain unverified or disconnected.
