# Scope

## Included in stage one

- Next.js App Router foundation with strict TypeScript and Tailwind CSS.
- Responsive application shell, design tokens and reusable UI primitives.
- Motion-ready homepage composed from independent static editorial sections.
- Catalog, category and product routes backed by a typed spreadsheet import and mock provider.
- URL-driven catalog filtering and sorting by category, gender, size, color, price and collection.
- Device-local cart with drawer, wishlist placeholder, checkout and account skeletons.
- Informational route structure, metadata, robots, sitemap and source-aware Product JSON-LD.
- Unit/component tests and Playwright smoke specifications.
- Architecture, design, accessibility, motion and performance documentation.

## Explicitly excluded

- Real inventory, customer, order, store, contact or legal data.
- Commerce backend, CMS, authentication, payments, transactional email and analytics.
- GSAP, canvas sequences, WebGL, cinematic video and production motion assets.
- Production deployment, redirects from the current site and data migration.

Stage two must not start until the backend choice, verified content and ownership of business data are decided.
